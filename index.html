Welcome to Guru Nanak Ji School & College

Guru Nanak Ji Public Model High School & College was established in 2000 by the Sardar Mastan Singh with the aim to educate his community childern. This is the unique School where Sikh children are getting both Gurumukhi and formal education. Now this School has been extended over 1500+ students belonging to sikh, Muslims, and Hindu and Christan communities.
